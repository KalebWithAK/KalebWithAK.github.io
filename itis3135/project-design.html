Project Requirements and Design

Project Name: 100 Black Women UNCC Website

Project Overview

  • This application will allow the members of 100 Black Women to showcase their club, upcoming/past events, and members. Additionally, the application will promote club growth with links to 100 Black Women's Instagram and ninerengage pages.
  • The intended users of the website are people interested in learning about or joining the 100BW club and members looking for upcoming events.
  • The website will feature:
    • A header with links to the different pages: home, events, committees, our women, and contact.
    • A footer with links to 100BW's Instagram and ninerengage pages.
    • A home page displaying the call to action, group photos, and a call to action to check out the different committees (with links).
    • An events page displaying a list of upcoming events and past events with details.
    • A committees page displaying the five committees: Health and Culture Awareness, Membership, Community Service, Fundraising, and Professional Development with a description and pictures if available.
    • An our women page with a group picture (preferably differs from the one on the home page), a section with pictures for board members, committee members, and regular members. Each member would also display their name and a quote if provided.
    • A contact page with links to 100BW's Instagram and ninerengage pages as well as the vice president's email.

Page Design

Home page

  • Purpose: Introduce the visitor to the club and persuade them to learn about the different committees or contact the club.
  • Audience/Users of page: visitors and members.
  • Content of the Page:
    • This is not a page asking users to enter data.
    • There are no data fields that need validation.
    • This page will contain links and hoverable cards.
    • When the user clicks on a link, they will be redirected to the corresponding page. When the user hovers over a card, more text will appear.
  • Components
    • Header
      • 100BW logo/link to home page
      • link to committees page
      • link to events page
      • link to our women page
      • link to contact page
    • Mission statement - paragraph about the club
    • Group photo - image
    • Events card - text and link to events page
    • Committees card - text and link to committees page
    • Footer
      • This page was designed and developed by Kaleb text
      • Link to contact page
